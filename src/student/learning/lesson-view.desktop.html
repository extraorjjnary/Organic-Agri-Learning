Green Materials: The Nitrogen Source

Learn which kitchen scraps and garden waste add vital nitrogen to your pile. Nitrogen is the "protein" of the compost heap, fueling the bacteria that do the heavy lifting.

check_circle What counts as Green?

  • add Vegetable and fruit scraps
  • add Coffee grounds and tea bags
  • add Fresh grass clippings

cancel Avoid these items

  • remove Dairy products and oils
  • remove Meat or fish bones
  • remove Pet waste (cats/dogs)
